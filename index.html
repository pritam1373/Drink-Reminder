Why Hydration is Important?

⚡ Boosts Energy
▼

Water helps transport oxygen and nutrients to active cells. Mild dehydration can drop performance, cause lethargy, and cloud mental focus.

💧 Better Skin
▼

Proper skin hydration maintains elasticity, prevents dryness, and flushes toxins from your body for a healthy, glowing appearance.

🎯 Improves Focus
▼

Brain tissue is about 75% water. Dehydration shrinks tissue volume, leading to persistent headaches and attention deficit.

❤️ Healthy Body
▼

Water plays a crucial role in maintaining fluid balances, regulating internal temperatures, and assisting kidney digestive tracking.
